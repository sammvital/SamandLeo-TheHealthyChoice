Ingredients For A Healthy Lifestyle

A healthy eating pattern includes a variety of fruits and vegetables, fat-free or low fat dairy, various protein foods, and oils. It also means limiting saturated fats, trans fats, added sugars, and salt.

One of the hardest parts of eating healthy and making these recipes is finding the right ingredients. Don't worry we got you covered! Below are the best local farmer markets with the freshest ingredients.

Downtown LA

Historic Downtown Farmers' Market is the neighborhood's only Certified Farmers' Market. The weekly market features fresh produce, local food and artisans, and live music.

Sundays 9am - 3pm; W. 5th St. (between S. Spring & S. Broadway), Los Angeles 90013

South LA

Central Avenue Farmers Market - This market features a small group of farmers that sell a variety of farm-fresh produce, nuts and dried fruits, and family-friendly events like healthy cooking demos, arts & crafts workshops, and seasonal activities for the entire community.

Thursdays 9am – 2pm; 4301 S. Central Ave, Los Angeles 90011

Eastside

The Atwater Village Farmers’ Market hosts more than two dozen vendors, including Tutti Frutti Farms, best known for heirloom tomatoes and other organic produce; organic herbs and lettuces from Kenter Canyon Farms; and La Bahn Ranch, purveyors of fresh eggs and other poultry.

Sundays 9am – 2pm; 3528 Larga Ave, Los Angeles 90039

Easy Tips for Living a Healthy Life

1. Make healthy food choices.

Eating healthier can be easier than you think, making simple changes such as adding vegetable pizza toppings or sprinkling more peppers, onions, and tomato in your breakfast omelet can make a difference!

2. Add at least one more vegetable to each meal.

Adding veggies to a meal doesn't have to be boring make it fun! You can do this by making a tasty dip such as hummus or guacamole. Even adding broccoli or peas to Mac & Cheese counts (and is delicious).

3. Build physical activity into your daily routines.

Again, this does not need to be drastic simply taking the stairs instead of the elevator at work or walking to the local Starbucks instead of driving and you will feel a difference.
